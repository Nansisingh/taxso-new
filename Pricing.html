Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is TaxSo?

TaxSo is a web based inventory management and taxation software. It is meant for use by distributors, suppliers, stockists, manufacturers, etc. who are looking for an easy to use, reliable and affordable solution to their inventory control and accounting requirements.

2. What is a customized plan?

TaxSo consists of multiple configurable modules. While a user is given an option to buy a defult premium plan, they are also allowed to pick and choose these configurable modules and integrate them to create a customized plan as per their need.

3. From where can I buy TaxSo?

You can book a demo Here or you can give us a call on +91-7017179208. Our Sales team will get in touch to give you a free demo.

4. Is TaxSo GST compliant?

GST became effective from 1st July 2017. TaxSo is fully GST enabled and its reports are in compliance with GST norms.

5. Who can operate this software?

TaxSo has been built keeping user friendliness in mind. Anyone who has basic knowledge of internet and web surfing can work on this software. No prior accounting experience is required. It is extremely simple and easy to use.

6. Can I terminate my subscription before plan expiry?

Yes, you can terminate your service at any time. You will get your refund after deducting the usage charges. Usage charges will be determined by calculating the number of months the service has been used.

7. What about training?

We provide one week of online training for clients. Apart from that, our support team is available to respond to any technical issues that you might face during the subscription period.

8. Is my data safe online?

Yes, we value your data immensely. We perform weekly back-up on the servers to keep your valuable data safe and secure for the entire duration of your subscription period.
